Best Practices for Scanning the Supporting Documents

Scan Settings:

  • Set the Scan Clarity to at least 300 DPI.
  • Choose the format of saving the document as PDF and name the document appropriately.
  • Scan the document in Black and White only.
  • Please ensure that the document is not password protected.

Scanning Source Documents:

  • Scan only the original document.
  • Ensure that the document size is A4 or letter size only.
  • If there are multiple pages, kindly scan all the pages into a single file and ensure that the final size of the PDF does not exceed more than 10 MB.

To avoid poor quality:

  • Please ensure the original Document that is being scanned is clear and not faded.
  • If the documents are handwritten, please ensure that it is clear and legible.
  • Please ensure documents are free from ink bleeding or smudging.
